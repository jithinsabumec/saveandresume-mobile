Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Save & Resume mobile app (also displayed in some builds as Timestamp Saver).

Effective date: February 27, 2026

Save & Resume helps users save YouTube timestamps, organize them into categories, and reopen videos later at the saved moment. This policy explains, in simple terms, what data the app handles, how that data is used, and when that data is shared.

Important: Google Play requires this policy to be posted at a public web address. This HTML file is the policy text itself, but you still need to host it on a public URL before adding it to Play Console.

1. Information We Collect

The app may handle the following information:

  • Google account information: if you sign in with Google, the app may receive basic account information such as your name, email address, profile photo, and account identifier through Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.
  • Saved timestamp data: the app stores the YouTube video ID, source link, timestamp values, formatted timestamp text, category assignments, optional notes, and related save dates.
  • Shared link content: when you share a YouTube link to the app from Android, the app reads the text you shared so it can extract the video link and timestamp.
  • Video metadata: the app may request a YouTube video title and thumbnail image so the saved entry can be shown properly in the app.
  • Basic diagnostic information: the app currently uses basic local console logging for troubleshooting during app operation. It does not currently use a dedicated third-party analytics or crash-reporting service in the mobile codebase.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information above to:

  • sign you in and keep your account session active,
  • save and organize your YouTube timestamps and categories,
  • show video titles and thumbnails for the links you save,
  • open YouTube again at the correct saved time, and
  • maintain and troubleshoot the app.

3. Where Your Information Is Stored

Saved account and timestamp data is stored using Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. This means your saved information may be processed and stored on infrastructure operated by Google or Google-affiliated service providers.

4. Third-Party Services and Sharing

The app uses third-party services that may process certain data in order for the app to work:

  • Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication: used to authenticate users.
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: used to store saved timestamps, categories, and related app state.
  • YouTube endpoints: used to fetch video title and thumbnail information for a shared YouTube link.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not include a mobile advertising SDK in the current app codebase.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your saved timestamp data for as long as it remains in your account or until it is deleted. Inside the app, you can delete timestamps and categories directly. Signing out of the app does not automatically erase data already stored in your account.

If you want to request deletion of account-related data that is not removable inside the app, please use the public account deletion instructions page here: Delete Account.

6. Security

We rely on the security features of Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and standard secure web connections to help protect data in transit. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Children’s Privacy

Save & Resume is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this app.

8. International Data Processing

Because the app uses Google and Firebase services, your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on how those services operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date shown at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, data deletion requests, or other policy-related inquiries, please use the developer contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing for Save & Resume.

11. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Third-party services used by the app may have their own privacy policies. You can review Google’s privacy information here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
