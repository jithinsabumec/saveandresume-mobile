Delete Your Account

This page explains how users of Save & Resume (also shown in some builds as Timestamp Saver) can request deletion of their app account and associated data.

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Important: This process deletes your Save & Resume app account and app data. It does not delete your Google account.

How to Request Deletion

Step 1 Find the developer contact email shown on the Google Play Store listing for Save & Resume.
Step 2 Send an email with the subject line: Delete my Save & Resume account
Step 3 In the email, include the Google email address you used to sign in to the app so we can identify the correct account.
Step 4 We will process the deletion request and remove the associated app account data.

Please do not send passwords. Only send the email address used to sign in to the app and a clear deletion request.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • your app account record used for Save & Resume,
  • saved YouTube timestamps,
  • categories you created,
  • optional notes attached to saved timestamps, and
  • related app profile data stored for your use of Save & Resume.

What Data May Be Kept

We may keep limited records only when reasonably necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or to document that a deletion request was completed.

Retention Period

Account and app data requested for deletion will be deleted within a reasonable period after the request is verified. Limited records kept for legal or security reasons may be retained only for as long as necessary for those purposes.

Delete Some Data Without Deleting the Full Account

Inside the app, users can already remove individual timestamps and categories without deleting the full account.
